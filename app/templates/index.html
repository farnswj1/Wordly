Wordly

How to play

Guess the word in 6 tries.

Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word.

The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

  • A letter in the correct spot will be green.
  • A letter in the word, but in the wrong spot will be yellow.
  • A letter not in the word will be light gray.
